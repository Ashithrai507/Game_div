## 💡 Tips for Success

✈️ **Flying Tips:**
- Use smooth, gentle mouse movements
- Start high (500+ feet) to explore
- Check altitude gauge constantly
- Monitor fuel percentage

🛬 **Landing Tips:**
- Use radar to find airports (green squares)
- Approach from straight ahead
- Slow down to < 100 knots
- Descend gradually
- Watch for orange landing indicator
- Press SPACE when ready

⛽ **Fuel Management:**
- You start with 100% fuel
- Fuel decreases constantly while flying
- Land when fuel gets low (< 30%)
- Landing refills fuel to 100%

🎮 **Control Tips:**
- Center your mouse for level flight
- Small movements = smooth control
- Hold position to maintain attitude
- Scroll slowly for gentle speed changes
